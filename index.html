About ESPADA AFRICA

Our Mission in Bungoma County

ESPADA (Empowerment and Support Program for Adolescent Development in Africa) is a community-based organization dedicated to reducing adolescent pregnancy in Bungoma County, Kenya through integrated health, education, and protection interventions.

Founded in 2025 in response to the alarming rise in teenage pregnancies, we work directly with communities in the most affected sub-counties: Kimilili, Bumula, Webuye East, and Mt. Elgon.

Our approach combines evidence-based strategies with community-driven solutions to create sustainable change and empower young girls to reach their full potential.

Our Programs

Youth Health Services

Establishing Youth Health Corners in hospitals and conducting mobile clinics to provide adolescent-friendly reproductive health services and counseling throughout Bungoma County.

Comprehensive Education

Implementing age-appropriate sexuality education in schools and communities to empower adolescents with knowledge and life skills for informed decisions.

Child Protection

Strengthening community-based systems to prevent and respond to gender-based violence, sexual abuse, and child marriage through training and awareness.

Girl Empowerment

Creating Girl Empowerment Clubs and providing vocational training to build economic resilience, leadership skills, and self-confidence among young women.
